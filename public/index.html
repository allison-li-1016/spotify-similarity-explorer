Artists are being sued over similar-sounding songs

In the age of TikTok songs and democratization of music production, some big artists are being sued for similar-sounding songs. Notable instances include:

  • Dua Lipa
  • Olivia Rodrigo
  • Sam Smith

    • What makes songs sound similar to each other?

    There are many criteria that influence songs, such as tempo, energy, time signature, and instruments. A less measurable feature includes lyrics themselves, such as in the case of Dua Lipa's song "Levitating." All these factors contribute to making a song recognizable. To demonstrate the similarity between songs, you can select song criteria below to see the top 10 similar songs based on the selected features.
